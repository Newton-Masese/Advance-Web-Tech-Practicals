Watching Series

I enjoy watching series because good storytelling keeps you hooked. A strong plot, interesting characters, and unpredictable moments make a series worth binge-watching.

A good series is perfect for relaxing after work, studying, or the gym.

What I like in a good series

  • Good pacing: not too slow, not rushed.
  • Strong characters: believable personalities and growth.
  • Plot twists: moments that surprise you.
  • World-building: a setting that feels real and consistent.

Genres I watch the most

These are the categories I usually go for.

Action / Thriller

High energy, suspense, and intense moments.

Drama

Good character development and emotional depth.

Sci-Fi / Fantasy

Creative worlds, powers, and mind-bending stories.

My watchlist style

I usually pick series using a simple checklist:

  • Interesting trailer or first episode
  • Good reviews (but I still decide for myself)
  • Not too many filler episodes
  • Strong cast / characters
  • A satisfying ending (or ongoing but worth it)

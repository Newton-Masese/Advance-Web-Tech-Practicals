Gaming

Gaming is one of my favourite ways to relax, compete, and connect with friends. I enjoy games that have good progression, teamwork, and exciting challenges.

A quick session can be a great way to unwind after a busy day.

Why I enjoy gaming

  • Challenge: improving skills and learning strategies.
  • Social: playing online with friends or meeting new people.
  • Creativity: exploring stories, worlds, and different playstyles.
  • Variety: from quick matches to long story campaigns.

Favourite genres

These are the styles of games I usually enjoy the most.

Competitive / Online

Fast matches, teamwork, and skill improvement over time.

Story / Adventure

Immersive storylines, exploration, and memorable characters.

Sports / Simulation

Football games and realistic gameplay that feels rewarding.

Top games I enjoy

A simple list (this changes depending on what I’m playing recently).

  1. Football / Sports titles
    Competitive matches, building teams, improving skills.
    Fav
  2. Story-based games
    Good plot and progression keeps me engaged.
  3. Online multiplayer games
    Great for playing with friends and learning teamwork.
  4. Open-world exploration
    Freedom to explore and discover things at your own pace.
  5. Casual / quick games
    Perfect when I only have 15–30 minutes.

My “good session” checklist

A few small things that make a gaming session better.

  • Take short breaks to stay focused
  • Play with friends for better teamwork
  • Try one new strategy each session
  • Keep volume comfortable (especially late)
  • Stop when it stops being fun

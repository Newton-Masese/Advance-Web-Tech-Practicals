Lifestyle

Outside of gaming, football, and series, I like to go to the gym, hang out with friends, and listen to music. These things help me stay balanced and keep my energy up.

The gym helps with discipline, confidence, and overall health.

Gym

I enjoy going to the gym because it improves my fitness and helps me stay consistent. Even a short workout can make a big difference.

Example weekly split

  • Push: chest, shoulders, triceps
  • Pull: back, biceps
  • Legs: quads, hamstrings, calves
  • Core: abs + stability work

Hanging out with friends

Spending time with friends keeps life fun and helps me reset after studying or working.

Things we do

  • Watch football or highlights together
  • Go out for food or coffee
  • Game nights / online matches
  • Trips and chilled walks

Why it matters

  • Reduces stress
  • Boosts motivation
  • Makes good memories
  • Helps maintain balance

Listening to music

Music is part of my day — it helps with focus, mood, and energy. I listen while travelling, studying, and working out.

What I listen to

  • Focus music while studying
